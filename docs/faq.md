## General

**Is Open Camera free?**  
Yes, the Open Camera App is completely free to use as it is open-source software.

**What is Open Camera used for?**  
The Open Camera App is used for taking photos and recording videos on Android devices, offering advanced features such as manual controls, HDR, and various shooting modes.

**Does Open Camera work offline?**  
Yes, the Open Camera App can be used offline, as it does not require an internet connection to function.

---

## Download & Installation

**How do I download Open Camera?**  
You can download the Open Camera App from the Google Play Store or directly from the official website. Simply search for "Open Camera App" in your preferred source.

**Is Open Camera available on Windows / macOS / Linux?**  
No, the Open Camera App is specifically designed for Android devices and is not available for Windows, macOS, or Linux.

**Can I use Open Camera without installing anything?**  
No, you must install the Open Camera App on your Android device to use its features.

## Account & Pricing

**What's included in the free plan?**  
- High-quality photo and video capture  
- Manual controls (ISO, exposure, focus)  
- Various shooting modes (HDR, panorama, etc.)  
- Support for external microphones  
- No ads or in-app purchases  

**How do I upgrade to a paid plan?**  
Open Camera does not offer a paid plan, as the app is entirely free and open-source.

**Can I use Open Camera for commercial projects on the free plan?**  
Yes, you can use the Open Camera App for commercial projects as it is licensed under the GNU General Public License.

## Troubleshooting

**Open Camera is not loading — what should I do?**  
1. Restart your device.  
2. Clear the app cache in your device settings.  
3. Check for any available updates for the app.  
4. Reinstall the Open Camera App if the issue persists.  

**How do I contact support?**  
You can contact support for the Open Camera App through the official website's support section or by visiting the user community forums.
