The Open Camera App is a feature-rich, open-source photography application for Android devices, enhancing camera functionality.

## Option 1 — Browser / Web App

### Step 1 — Access the Google Play Store
1. Open the Google Play Store on your Android device.
2. Use the search bar to type "Open Camera".
3. Select the Open Camera App from the search results.

### Step 2 — Install the App
1. Tap on the "Install" button.
2. Wait for the installation process to complete.

### Step 3 — Open the App
1. Once installed, tap "Open" from the Play Store, or find the Open Camera icon in your app drawer.
2. Grant the necessary permissions for camera and storage access when prompted.

---

## Option 2 — Desktop App

### Step 1 — Visit the Open Camera Website
1. Open a web browser on your computer.
2. Go to the official Open Camera website: [https://opencamera.org.uk](https://opencamera.org.uk).

### Step 2 — Download the APK
1. Navigate to the "Download" section on the website.
2. Download the latest APK file for Open Camera.

---

!!! tip "Free Version"
    The Open Camera App is completely free and open-source, providing extensive features without any hidden costs.

!!! warning "Requirements"
    Ensure your Android device is running Android 4.0 (Ice Cream Sandwich) or higher and has at least 20 MB of free storage space for installation.
